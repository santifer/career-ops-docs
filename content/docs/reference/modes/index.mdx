## The 14 modes

career-ops ships **14 user-invocable modes**. Each one is a plain-markdown skill the agent loads and runs inside whichever AI coding CLI you already use — Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode, Gemini CLI, Qwen, or GitHub Copilot. You invoke a mode as `/career-ops <mode>`, and each covers one stage of the job search: evaluating a listing against the six-dimension rubric, tailoring a CV, drafting an application, tracking outcomes, or preparing for interviews. Modes are System Layer per the [Data Contract](https://github.com/santifer/career-ops/blob/main/DATA_CONTRACT.md) — they update with the tool and never modify your `cv.md`, profile, or reports.

## Evaluate

The core scoring loop — read a job listing and score it against the six dimensions before you spend any effort on it.

- **[auto-pipeline](/docs/reference/modes/auto-pipeline)** — the default flow. Paste a JD URL or text; it runs the A–F evaluation, writes the report, exports a tailored PDF, and logs a tracker entry with no manual steps in between.
- **[pipeline](/docs/reference/modes/pipeline)** — runs auto-pipeline against every URL queued in `data/pipeline.md`, so a day's worth of listings crunch in one session.
- **[oferta](/docs/reference/modes/oferta)** — the A–F evaluation against a single listing when you only need the scoring decision, not the full pipeline.
- **[ofertas](/docs/reference/modes/ofertas)** — ranks two or more competing offers across comp, fit, growth, and red flags into one decision matrix.
- **[deep](/docs/reference/modes/deep)** — a deep company-research document (funding, leadership, press, comp signals, red flags) before you apply to a company you don't know well.

## Tailor & apply

Turn a high-scoring match into a submitted application.

- **[pdf](/docs/reference/modes/pdf)** — renders a tailored, ATS-clean PDF résumé against a specific JD via Playwright.
- **[apply](/docs/reference/modes/apply)** — reads an open application form's open-ended questions and drafts answers grounded in your CV and the JD.
- **[contacto](/docs/reference/modes/contacto)** — surfaces likely-warm LinkedIn contacts and drafts a short, role-specific outreach message.
- **[followup](/docs/reference/modes/followup)** — flags applications and interviews overdue for follow-up and drafts the messages.

## Track & grow

Keep the search organised and calibrate it over time.

- **[tracker](/docs/reference/modes/tracker)** — a chat-side status summary of every application grouped by stage.
- **[patterns](/docs/reference/modes/patterns)** — reads your rejected and discarded applications to surface targeting patterns and blind spots.
- **[interview-prep](/docs/reference/modes/interview-prep)** — round-specific prep docs (recruiter, hiring manager, peer technical, panel) with likely questions and STAR stories.
- **[training](/docs/reference/modes/training)** — scores a course or certification against your stated career direction and its opportunity cost.
- **[project](/docs/reference/modes/project)** — scores a portfolio-project idea against your target roles and the time it would take to build.
